The credential gap finding

89 emerging task statements were identified across 12 sub-sectors and 10 technology trends. The current certification database contains 36 credentials. None directly cover the emerging task set.

Emerging work is forming faster than credential systems can track. This creates a practical assessment gap for employers and training providers — not because certification systems have failed, but because they characteristically lag new work.
