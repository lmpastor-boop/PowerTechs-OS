The four action labels

Hire Now
Demand is growing and structurally supported. Prioritize recruiting and near-term staffing.
Plan Ahead
Demand is rising but timing depends on policy or investment. Build pipeline now.
Monitor
Signal exists but direction or timing is mixed. Keep visible; avoid over-committing.
Phase Out
Demand is weakening or structurally declining. Redirect workers to adjacent roles.
